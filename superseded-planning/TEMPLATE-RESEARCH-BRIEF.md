# Template Research Brief: Last Apple Website Redesign

## Who We Are

**Last Apple** is a boutique digital solutions agency based in La Palma, California. We bring **30 years of system integration expertise** across enterprise environments (contact centers, healthcare, payment processing) to small and mid-market businesses who need that same level of rigor and care.

We're not a typical agency. We're **one person (Hank Groman) with deep technical expertise**, augmented by AI tools, delivering enterprise-quality work at boutique scale. We treat every client's website as mission-critical infrastructure, not just a marketing asset.

## What We Do

### Three Service Pillars:

**1. WordPress Care**
- Maintenance plans ($150-$500/month)
- Performance optimization
- Site resurrection (fixing broken/neglected sites)

**2. AI-Powered Marketing**
- Chatbots for lead qualification
- B2B email list intelligence
- AI-assisted content creation
- Social media automation

**3. System Integration**
- Data flow automation between tools
- HubSpot optimization
- CRM/marketing automation connections

## Our Clients

## Technical Requirements

The template/design system must be:

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Framework** | Next.js 14+ (App Router) |
| **Styling** | Tailwind CSS |
| **Components** | shadcn/ui compatible or similar |
| **Responsive** | Mobile-first, looks great on all devices |
| **Fast** | Static generation, optimized images |
| **Accessible** | WCAG compliant |
| **SEO-ready** | Proper semantic HTML, meta handling |

1. **Home** - Hero, services overview, social proof, CTA

3. **Stream/Blog** - List view + detail view for articles
4. **About** - Story, values, founder info
5. **Contact** - Form, contact info
6. **Consultation** - Booking/form page
